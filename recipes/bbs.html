Best Bean Salad



Description

Prep Time: 20 mins
Additional Time: 8 hrs
Total Time: 8 hrs 20 mins
Servings: 18
Yield: 12 cups


Ingredients

  • 1 (15.5 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can black beans, drained
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can green beans, drained
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can wax beans, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup salad oil
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine garbanzo beans, kidney beans, black beans, green beans, wax beans, green pepper, onion, and celery in a large bowl; toss to mix.

  2. Whisk together sugar, oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a separate bowl until sugar is dissolved; pour over bean mixture. Mix well.

  3. Refrigerate salad for 8 hours to overnight before serving.
